Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Where is Shibal Collections Boutique located?

We are located in Kampala at kampala Bouliverd building, Uganda — dealing in high-quality ladies’ wear and accessories. Visit our Contact page for detailed directions.

2. What types of products do you sell?

We specialize in trendy ladies' fashion including dresses, handbags, shoes, jewelry, and accessories for all occasions.

3. Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we provide fast and reliable delivery services within Kampala and nearby areas. Orders outside Kampala can be arranged via courier.

4. How can I place an order?

You can order directly through our website, social media pages, or WhatsApp. After placing an order, our team will confirm your request immediately.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel), cash on delivery (within Kampala), and bank payments for large orders.

6. Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes. Items can be returned or exchanged within 3 days of purchase if unused and in original condition. Please keep your receipt.

7. Do you sell wholesale items?

Absolutely! Shibal Collections offers wholesale deals for boutique owners and resellers. Contact us for pricing and details.
